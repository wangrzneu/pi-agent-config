# Architecture

Before proposing structural changes:

1. Define the goal, constraints, and non-goals.
2. Map the current boundaries, ownership, and data flow.
3. Compare at least two viable options.
4. Prefer the smallest reversible interface or seam.
5. Describe migration steps, tests, and rollback.
6. Call out assumptions and unresolved tradeoffs.

Keep the recommendation concrete and avoid speculative abstractions.
